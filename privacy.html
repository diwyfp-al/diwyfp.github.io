Privacy Policy

Data Security

Reasonable physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards are maintained to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

User Rights

Individuals may request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal information maintained by DIWY Financial Planning, subject to applicable laws and contractual obligations.
