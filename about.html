About DIWY Financial Planning

DIWY (pronounced "dee-wee", like “kiwi.”) stands for "Do-It-With-You" — a modern, collaborative approach to financial planning. You receive advice-only, flat-fee support tailored to your goals. There's no requirement to move assets, no product sales, and no pressure. Just thoughtful guidance from a CFP® professional who works with you to make confident financial decisions.

Why Choose DIWY?

  • Independent, fiduciary advice that puts your interests first
  • No commissions, no sales — just planning
  • Flexible, on-demand support when you need it
  • Collaborative technology-enabled delivery
  • Transparent flat-fee pricing

A Planning Model That Fits Your Life

DIWY Financial Planning is built for real people with real lives — busy, changing, and sometimes messy. You decide how much support you want, and when you want it. We’ll walk with you as a guide — not a gatekeeper.

DIWY Financial Planning (CRD #335615) is a Registered Investment Adviser (RIA) offering advisory services in the state of California and other jurisdictions where exempted.
