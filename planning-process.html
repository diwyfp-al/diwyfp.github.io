The DIWY Planning Process

Our approach is designed to make financial planning more accessible, less overwhelming, and more useful in real life. No need to do it all at once. Just what you need, when you need it.

Step 1: Introductory Meeting

We get to know each other, clarify expectations, and confirm whether there’s a good mutual fit.

Step 2: Orientation, Calibration, and Data Validation

We clarify your priorities, assess your current situation, and confirm the information that will support good planning.

Step 3: Implementation

We focus on your highest priorities, deliver specific recommendations, and help you move forward with confidence.

Ongoing Planning, On Your Terms

After the initial three-step process, you can access planning support on demand and as needed. The DIWY process is designed to align with how planning happens in real life—flexible, responsive, and based on what matters most to you at the moment.

There’s no need for comprehensive planning all at once, especially when it can be unnecessary or overwhelming. Get help when you need it, not ongoing fees when you don’t.
