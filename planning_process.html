How Our Planning Process Works

At DIWY Financial Planning, we meet you where you are and help you get where you're going—one clear step at a time. Our process is collaborative, modular, and on-demand.

Organize

We start by helping you get organized—gathering the right documents, listing your accounts, and understanding your goals, obligations, and priorities.

Evaluate

Together, we evaluate your current situation—cash flow, assets, liabilities, and risks—and assess what's working and what needs attention.

Plan

We clarify your goals, identify planning opportunities, and prioritize what matters most—so you can take action at your pace, with support as needed.
