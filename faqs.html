Frequently Asked Questions

How do you pronounce DIWY? What does it mean?

DIWY is pronounced "dee-wee", like the word “kiwi.”
It stands for Do It With You, because financial planning should be a collaborative experience.

Do I have to move my money or open new accounts?

No. DIWY is an advice-only firm. You keep control of your money; we offer objective guidance.

Do you sell products or earn commissions?

No. We are fee-only fiduciaries — no sales, no hidden agendas.

What do you charge?

Flat-fee model starting at $400/hr. You only pay for time spent in meetings, not behind-the-scenes work.

Who do you work with?

We serve people from all walks of life, especially self-directed clients who want a collaborative partner.

What if I just have a few quick questions?

You can book one-time sessions or schedule multiple meetings — your choice.

Do you meet virtually?

Yes! Phone and video meetings are available 7-days a week.

How do I get started?

Schedule a no-cost, no-obligation 30-minute call at this link.

What tools do you use?

Secure collaborative software for organizing, tracking, and planning. Includes screen sharing and visual tools.
