Disclosure Regarding Communication Recording and Retention

All communications with DIWY Financial Planning (DIWYFP) are subject to recording and retention for purposes of quality assurance, compliance monitoring, and satisfaction of regulatory requirements.

This includes, but is not limited to, responses to questionnaires, written correspondence (including email and text messages), telephone conversations, and meetings conducted virtually or in person.

By engaging with DIWYFP, you consent to such recording and retention. All such records are maintained in a secure manner consistent with applicable regulatory and industry standards.

Existing Clients Back to top

A 60-minute billable Zoom or phone meeting.

Mutual expectations

  • Be punctual
  • Avoid distractions and background noise
  • Be logged into the financial planning tool
  • Meetings are done via Zoom to foster collaboration. Cameras do not need to be turned on.

FAQs

  • Meetings are available 7 days a week. If a time shows as available, you may book it.
  • Client cancellations or reschedules with 48+ hours’ notice have no repercussions.
  • Client changes within 48 hours or no-shows may be subject to the following:
    • First meeting of an engagement: No fee, but your 30-day engagement window begins on the originally scheduled date.
    • Later meetings: The next meeting begins a new engagement window with billing and discounts reset to meeting #1.
  • If DIWY Financial Planning cancels or reschedules:
    • Your rescheduled meeting is free,
    • Your 30-day engagement window resets, and
    • All meetings in the new 30-day engagement window are billed at the lowest hourly rate stated in your Financial Planning Agreement.
