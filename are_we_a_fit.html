Are We a Fit?

At DIWY Planning, we believe in meaningful partnerships. We're not for everyone—and that's okay. We do our best work with people who are ready to actively participate in their financial journey.

You might be a good fit if:

  • You value collaboration and want a financial planning partner, not just a product pusher.
  • You’re a first-generation wealth builder or transitioning from DIY to getting professional guidance.
  • You prefer flat, transparent fees over hidden costs or asset-based pricing.
  • You’re open to honest conversations and ready to take action.

We might not be the best fit if:

  • You’re only looking for investment management or stock tips.
  • You’re seeking a one-size-fits-all financial product or quick fix.
  • You’re not ready to commit time to the planning process.
  • You’re not comfortable with virtual meetings or digital tools.

If you're still reading and nodding your head—chances are good we should talk.
