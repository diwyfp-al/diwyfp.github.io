Process, Services & Engagement Models

DIWY Financial Planning meets you where you are. Choose how you want to engage and when. Whether you're a self-directed investor or seeking personalized guidance, we provide tools, structure, and collaborative support that fits your needs.

Process

  1. Introductory Meeting: A no-obligation 30-minute call to see if there is a mutual fit. You'll also receive access to a planning tool to help guide our conversation.
  2. Orientation & Calibration: Based on your preferred level of support, we structure the engagement accordingly—whether it's self-directed, group-based, one-on-one, or a combination.
  3. Implementation & Monitoring: Once priorities are set, we move into action. You'll get clarity and accountability, with a plan to revisit as life evolves.
