Our Process – How We Work Together

We make financial planning approachable, flexible, and collaborative. Here’s what working with us looks like:

  1. Start With a Conversation: A no-pressure intro call to hear your story and understand your goals.
  2. Collaborative Planning: Work in 30-day windows. Each includes up to 3 virtual sessions, access to our planning portal, and unlimited support during the engagement.
  3. A Clear Financial Picture: We use RightCapital to visualize your full financial life, explore "what-if" scenarios, track cash flow, and more. You're always in control.
  4. Plan at Your Pace: Most clients schedule sessions about a week apart. You decide when to check in again—no subscriptions or pressure.
  5. Optional Document Upload: Securely share tax returns, paystubs, statements, and anything relevant via your personal RightCapital Vault.

Whether you're planning for a home, reducing debt, or setting up your future, we're here to help you move forward with confidence.
