Services & Engagement Levels

DIWY Financial Planning offers flexible support options to meet your needs. You can go it alone with professional tools, collaborate in a group, or work directly one-on-one. Our advice-only model is always fee-transparent and product-free.

Process

  1. Introductory Meeting: A no-pressure, no-obligation 30-minute introductory call to determine if there is a mutual fit. You will receive complimentary access to a financial planning tool to help facilitate our discussion.
  2. Orientation and Calibration: If you decide to move forward, the engagement model that you choose will determine next steps. You can take the self-directed route, attend group sessions, meet one-on-one or do a combination.
  3. Implementation and Monitoring: Once your goals are idetified and prioritized, we'll work on implementation and establish a plan for monitoring the plan over time.
